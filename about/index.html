About Nexus

Your trusted partner in every step of your transformation

Shaping the Future
of Organizations in MENA
Nexus Advisory is a regional innovative and well-versed boutique advisory professional services provider.

Our dedicated team of experts are committed to helping major public and private sector organizations across the MENA region to cater tailored solutions.

These solutions are designed to address their organizational context across multiple domains including strategic planning, organizational development, performance management, organizational transformation, business process management, capability building, and project management.
Partnering for Progress
in a Changing World
We at Nexus Advisory cherish the bold success of our partners.

We continually seek new and better ways in doing things, bringing the latest market trends and technologies to address your changing requirements and achieve better and faster outcomes.

Our experts combine a global mindset with a wealth of local experience in management consulting.

This experience is acquired through working with global corporations, government entities, and organizations across various industries. We tackle complex challenges and drive large-scale transformations.

Proven Success in National
& Regional Strategic Initiatives
We have successfully accomplished several engagements on regional and national initiatives that involved partnering with multiple stakeholders to develop comprehensive strategies and execute transformational projects.

Our previous experience with the Ministry of Culture & Youth in developing the operating model and detailing the structure of the National Orchestra demonstrates our deep understanding of the music industry trends, challenges, and opportunities both regionally and internationally.
